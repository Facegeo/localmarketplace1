    Frequently Asked Questions

    Have questions about our services? Check out the answers to the most commonly asked questions below.

    1. How does your platform connect farmers with consumers?

    We facilitate direct connections between farmers and consumers through our online platform, ensuring fair trade practices. By cutting out middlemen, we allow farmers to sell their produce at better prices, while consumers get fresh products at affordable rates.

    2. What types of products can I buy on your platform?

    Our platform offers a wide range of agricultural products, including fresh fruits, vegetables, grains, and dairy products. We are constantly expanding our inventory based on consumer demand.

    3. How do I place an order?

    Simply visit our website, browse the available products, and add them to your cart. Once you are ready, proceed to checkout where you can complete your order using a secure payment method.

    4. What is your return policy?

    We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. If there are any issues with the product, please contact our support team for assistance. We strive to ensure customer satisfaction with every purchase.

    5. How can I get in touch with customer support?

    You can contact our customer support team by visiting the Contact page on our website or by emailing us at support@farmtoconnect.com. We will respond to your inquiries as quickly as possible.

    6. Are there any subscription plans for using the platform?

    Currently, we offer the platform to all users free of charge. In the future, we may introduce premium features, such as subscription-based delivery services for regular customers.

    7. Can I sell my products on the platform?

    Yes, you can register as a seller on our platform and list your agricultural products. We ensure that all sellers meet our quality and ethical standards to guarantee the best experience for consumers.

    8. How do you ensure product quality?

    We have strict quality control measures in place. Our team works closely with farmers to ensure that all products meet health and safety standards. Additionally, we encourage customers to provide feedback on product quality to maintain high standards.

    9. Is the platform available internationally?

    Currently, our platform is only available in select regions. However, we are working to expand our services internationally, so stay tuned for updates on availability in your area!

    10. How can I track my order?

    You can track your order by visiting the "My Orders" section on our website, where you will find real-time tracking updates. You will also receive email notifications on the status of your order.
